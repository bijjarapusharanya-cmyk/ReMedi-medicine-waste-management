ReMedi is a concept-based application and offline platform designed to reduce medicine wastage while making healthcare more affordable. It enables users to donate unused or expired medicines and receive reward coupons that can be used to purchase medicines at discounted prices on the ReMedi platform.

The system ensures that usable medicines reach NGOs and orphanages, while expired medicines are safely redirected to research or fertilizer centers, creating a complete and sustainable medicine lifecycle.

## â— Problem Statement
Large quantities of unused and expired medicines are thrown away every year, leading to environmental pollution and resource wastage. At the same time, many people struggle to afford essential medicines. Currently, there is no unified platform that safely manages medicine donation, recycling, and reuse while also incentivizing users to participate.
